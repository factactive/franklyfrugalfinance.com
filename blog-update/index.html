Blog Update

Posted on September 16, 2019 by Kipp

I will try to not do too many of these, as my focus is primarily on content, but I wanted to let all of my readers know about the pages I have been adding recently as a quick reference to reoccurring articles.

Additionally I have signed up with twitter and facebook.  Although, it is worth mentioning that I am not a big fan of facebook and I haven’t been updating that regularly… yet.. but I would probably be more motivated if I had some followers!  That and I haven’t found a way to auto post my latest posts onto the page yet, but I have found a way to do so for twitter but honestly I was doing good on twitter before the auto post thing.

So here is a quick breakdown of the pages I have added or updated recently:

About

Although an About page has existed since I created this website, I went back and revamped it a little bit and tried to tie into articles that I think are relevant to learning more about me, my plans, and my goals.

Sub-Page from About: Contact

This is just another way for people to get in touch with me.  At first I was going to not do this as a sub-page, but my current theme would create a new row for a 7th page so I decided to nest it under about.  Plus I am not currently seeking freelancing or other employment so I don’t need a neon hire me sign – not yet anyway.

Net Worth

This page is what it says it is.  Just a one stop shop for people wanting to snoop around and see how much I am worth.  Don’t get too excited, I don’t have 6 figures to my name, but I do plan on changing that in the near future!

Savings Rates

How much you save is key to obtaining financial independence at an early age.  So my monthly battles are shown here along with the first post being the first 1/2 of this year.  The reason I lumped it all together is because I started blogging in July and I wanted to complete the year for year-to-year comparisons in the future.  The great thing about this being in percent is that it is easy to compare.  Sure circumstances can change over time, but the percentage one saves is key to success over the long haul.

Frugality and Resources

You can call it my sales page if you prefer.  It is basically a few things, some of which from other blogs, where I have implemented a specific frugal strategy, a few amazon affiliates links to books or products that I find useful in saving money, and also any articles I write about frugal living.  This is intended to be a quick reference page to help visitors find resources that can help save money or find some good reads.  I would recommend checking your local library first for books, that is what your taxes are for anyways.

Portfolio

My portfolio exposed!  I have throughout my stock purchase articles talked about all of my individual holdings, but now those can be viewed all in one place, and it should automatically update to the markets.  It appears that on occasion it will just show as “loading” as maybe the connection to the tables isn’t always synced.  But it is a very nice system overall.

So Why are you Blogging about Blogging?

Well, any other blogger will tell you that all of this back round stuff takes time.  And I being that I have limited time, when I take time away from blogging to do these other things to improve the blog, I don’t necessarily have time to write as well.  That and I probably need to prioritize my time better.  It always feels that I don’t have enough free time and I need to keep working towards financial independence to obtain more of it.

I do not plan on doing these types of update often.  I will more than likely have a theme change in the future, but everything is functional for now.  It is something that I desire to change in the future and may or may not write about it at the time.  A theme change is usually quite obvious to visitors while these page updates may slip by if you go straight to the articles.
